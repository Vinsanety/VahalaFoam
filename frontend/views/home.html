About Vahala Foam


Vahala Foam Inc. has a long history in the foam industry. Dating back to the 1960's the Vahala family owned and operated a foam fabrication business which sold in 1987.

In 1991, Dave and Dan Vahala committed to a new idea of making a better business which focuses on being a top end foam supplier that provides the highest quality product with superior service at a competitive price. Throughout the years we have advanced in size and capabilities allowing us to provide our clients with the best in service, quality and price.

Capabilities

At Vahala Foam, Inc. customization is a strategic factor to designing quality products. Our team of design experts are educated and experienced in the latest technology.
We have the ability to develop customized products from the uncomplicated to the complex. We believe in devoting our knowledge, talent and resources to providing you with the best possible solution. One of our strengths is the dedication to working along side our clients to develop a superior product that not only offers support, but is also comfortable and durable.

VF's Frequently Asked Questions


  • question_answerQ. If I have an idea for a seat, chair or fitting any structure with foam, what do you need from me to get the procedure started?

    A. Pictures and dimensions of frame. Any sketches of finished product will get the procedure started.

  • question_answerQ. Do you consult as to what types of foam to use for a certain application?

    A. Yes. We can guide you with the design and the comfort level to give you the best price for your product.

  • question_answerQ. Do you sell to the public?

    A. Yes, absolutely. All inquiries are welcomed.

  • question_answerQ. Do you deal with Styrofoam? What kind of foam to you deal with?

    A. Yes. We have a source for this product. We deal with polyurethane, polyethylene, Visco Elastic (memory foam)

  • question_answerQ. Can you cut the foam?

    A. Yes. We have the ability to cut and glue to fit your size applications.

  • question_answerQ. Do you have standard sizes?

    A. No. We will cut to accommodate your order size.

  • question_answerQ. Do you deliver and what is your delivery area?

    A. Yes. Primarily in the states of IN, MI, OH and IL.
